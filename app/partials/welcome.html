Trades

To buy ten stocks in Greencross Ltd, simply begin typing either the stock code (GXL) or the company name in the search box.

Basic price information and charting is loaded automatically, while chart overlays and information such as market depth or relavant news can also be found in the lower section of the page.

Portfolio

After you place the order, you can view and cancel the pending trade by going to your portfolio. The trade is queued for execution, which occurs periodically. You can also view your current holdings, the historical performance of your full portfolio, and whether your trades have made money yet.

Brokerage


Each trade incurs a brokerage fee, just like the real stock exchange

Pricing Detail


Current Price reflects the last-executed trade's value (per stock), High and Low are the maximum and minimum price the stock has sold for today, Open was the price of the stock when the exchange opened and Previous Close was the price at 4pm the business day before today. Finally, some companies will pay their shareholders periodic Dividends - the last dividend payment date and amount (per share) are shown in the pricing.
